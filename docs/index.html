SLMs Improved With Local Sufficiency
arXiv · ●●●○○ Significant
›

Researchers found that small language models can be improved by leveraging local sufficiency, where the preferred token of a larger language model is often within the top-K predictions of the smaller model. This insight led to the development of SELECT TO THINK, a method that simplifies the supervision signal to discrete candidate rankings.

This matters because it enables more efficient and scalable deployment of language models by mitigating the reasoning power gap between small and large language models.
