Sessa Attention Model
arXiv · ●●●○○ Significant
›

Sessa is a new decoder that integrates attention into a feedback path, allowing for recurrent many-path aggregation within a layer. This approach addresses the limitations of existing architectures, which either retrieve from the past in a single read or propagate information through a single feedback chain.

Sessa's novel attention mechanism has the potential to improve the performance of sequence models, particularly in tasks that require long-range dependencies and complex contextual understanding.
